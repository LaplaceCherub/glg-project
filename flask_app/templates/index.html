We are Ying, Cody and Cris, students of FourthBrain's Machine Learning Engineer course, cohort 9 (August-December 2022) and this is our capstone project.

GLG's business largely revolves around matching clients with topic experts so that they can meet by phone, video or in person.

To do that a client submits a request describing the topic(s) they seek to gain expertise on and GLG in turn searches their database of 1 million experts for the right match so that the meeting can be scheduled in short order.

Our web app uses natural language processing (NLP) to quickly and accurately identify the key topic(s) contained in each client request so that the matching process can be semi-automated via machine learning.

For an in-depth and technical look, we invite you to also visit our Github repository.

To test drive our web app, simply go to the "Try it Out" tab and enter your own request for topic expertise.

Thanks for visiting.
